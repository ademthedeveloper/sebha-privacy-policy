The Concept of Amanah (Trust)

At Dev360, we view software development not just as a service, but as an Amanah—a sacred trust. When you use the Sebha app, you are trusting us with your time and your spiritual focus. We honor this trust by ensuring that your digital footprint remains entirely yours. We believe that privacy is a fundamental right, and in the context of worship, it is even more paramount.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sebha application operates and why we have chosen to collect zero data. We have built this tool to be a silent companion in your dhikr, ensuring that no commercial or analytical noise interferes with your remembrance of the Almighty.

01

Absolute Privacy: Zero Data Policy

Our commitment to your privacy is absolute. We do not use any tracking technologies, and we do not maintain any servers that store user information. Your devotion is personal, and it should remain that way.

✦

No Identity Tracking: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or social media profiles.

✦

No Hardware Identifiers: We do not track your device's IMEI, UUID, or MAC address.

✦

No Location Data: The app does not request access to your GPS or IP-based location.

✦

No Usage Analytics: We do not track how many times you open the app or which buttons you click.

02

Local Storage & Sovereignty

Any data generated by the app, such as your current tasbih count or set goals, is stored locally on your own device.

  • "Local storage means your data never leaves your palm."
  • This data is not synced to any cloud service.
  • If you delete the app, your local counts are permanently erased from your device.
  • We have no way to "back up" or "restore" your data because we never see it.
03

A Sanctuary Without Ads

Spiritual focus requires a clear mind and a heart free from worldly distractions. We have made a firm commitment to keep this app 100% ad-free.

Unlike many apps that use "free" services in exchange for your data to show you ads, Sebha is truly free. We do not include any third-party advertising SDKs (Software Development Kits) from providers like Google, Meta, or Unity.

No Banners • No Interstitials • No Commercial Tracking
04

Device Permissions

To provide the best experience, the app may request minimal system-level permissions. These are used only for the functionality you see:

  • Haptic Feedback & Sound

    The app uses your device's vibration motor and speakers to provide tactile and auditory feedback when a count is recorded. This stays on your device.

  • Notifications

    If enabled, the app may send local reminders for dhikr. These are scheduled on your phone and do not involve an external server.

05

Safe for All Generations

Our app is intentionally designed to be a safe space for every member of the family, including children and the elderly.

No Interaction

No chat features, public leaderboards, or profile sharing. Users cannot contact each other through the app.

Wholesome Content

100% focused on spiritual dhikr. No external links, news feeds, or unpredictable content.

06

Global Standards

While many regulations like GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California) focus on how data is managed, we exceed these standards by simply not collecting data at all. Your rights to access, delete, or port your data are naturally satisfied because the data never leaves your control.

A Sincere Conclusion
